## Do not jam

**Intentionally interfering with radio communications is illegal** in most countries and may be a criminal offense. It can disrupt emergency, aviation, medical, and consumer systems.

This repository must **not** be used as instructions to:

- Build or operate jammers  
- Attack networks or devices you do not own  
- Cause harmful interference
